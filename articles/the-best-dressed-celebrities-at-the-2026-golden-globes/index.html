The Best-Dressed Celebrities at the 2026 Golden Globes

February 15, 2026 - 3 min read

The 2026 Golden Globes lit up the Beverly Hilton as Hollywood's biggest names arrived dressed to impress. From the moment stars stepped onto the red carpet, it was clear the evening would celebrate both cinematic excellence and unforgettable style. Hosted by comedian Nikki Glaser, the ceremony blended sharp humor with the kind of old-school glamour the Golden Globes do best.

Fashion That Embraced the Drama

When it came to fashion, presenters and nominees fully embraced the theatricality of awards season. Bold colors, sculptural silhouettes, and an abundance of diamonds dominated the night. This was not about playing it safe — it was about making a genuine entrance and letting the clothes rise to the occasion.

Craftsmanship Takes Center Stage

Many of the most talked-about looks were the result of extraordinary craftsmanship. Several custom designs took months to complete, reinforcing the importance of slow fashion and genuine artistry at fashion's highest levels.

Selena Gomez's black velvet Chanel bustier gown, adorned with more than 200 handcrafted floral details made from feathers, organza, and silk chiffon, required 323 hours of meticulous work. Amanda Seyfried's white strapless Versace gown, paired with a dramatic stole, took an impressive 400 hours to create. These weren't just dresses — they were works of art as carefully constructed as the performances being honored inside the ballroom.

Surprise Moments That Stole the Spotlight

The night also delivered fashion surprises that became immediate talking points. Sinners star Wunmi Mosaku made a powerful statement in a vibrant yellow Matthew Reisman gown while joyfully announcing her second pregnancy — a red-carpet moment that felt genuinely celebratory. Odessa A'Zion, nominated for Marty Supreme, embraced vintage glamour in a black feathered Dolce & Gabbana coat, bringing classic Hollywood drama to the modern carpet.

The Gentlemen Held Their Own

The men of the evening were equally memorable. Heated Rivalry co-stars turned heads, with Hudson Williams standing out in a crisp white Giorgio Armani tuxedo complete with a cummerbund. Many of the night's best-dressed men opted for timeless tailoring elevated with bold accessories, including brooches worn by Michael B. Jordan in Prada and Jeremy Allen White in Louis Vuitton — proving that menswear on the red carpet has entered an era of genuine creativity.

Conclusion

The 2026 Golden Globes red carpet was a study in balance: craftsmanship alongside bold personal style, timeless elegance alongside genuine surprise. From the hundreds of hours poured into custom gowns to the smaller, more intimate fashion moments, the evening reminded us that awards season fashion is at its best when it tells a story — and every look on this red carpet had something to say.
